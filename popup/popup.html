Why This Matters

Every AI prompt consumes real energy and water. A single ChatGPT query uses 10x more energy than a Google search. Image generation uses 60x more.

Globally, AI data centers are projected to consume as much electricity as entire countries by 2027. They already use billions of liters of fresh water for cooling.

This isn't about stopping — it's about being aware. Use AI intentionally. Combine questions. Skip regenerations when the first answer works. Small choices, collectively, shape the footprint.
